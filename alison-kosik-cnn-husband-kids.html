It looks like the CNN personality Alison Kosik prefers to keep her personal life away from the media eyes. However, the sources suggest that the award-winning journalist is married to banker husband and is a mother of a son.

Alison Kosik is an American journalist currently serving as a Business Correspondent for CNN, reporting from the New York Stock Exchange. Besides, she appears as a co-anchor on CNN’s morning show, Early Start and is a fill-in host for CNN International’s daily morning business show.

Alison Kosik Bio; Parents, Education, Height

Alison was born on 28 April 1971 to parents Gilbert Kosik and Brenda Kosik. She grew up with her family in Miami, Florida.

Standing at the height of ‎5 feet 5 inches (1.65 meters), she attended Nova High School. Later, she went to The American University and graduated with a B.A. in Broadcast Journalism and Political Science.

Her Career; CNN, Salary

Alison began her career as a reporter for CBS-affiliated KZTV-TV and NBC-affiliated KRIS-TV. Afterward, she took the responsibility as a reporter and fill-in anchor for WPEC-TV/WFLX-TV West Palm Beach, Florida. Moreover, she was a business correspondent for EnergyNewsLive.com, covering the energy sector, energy policy and the politics of the energy industry from Capitol Hill.
