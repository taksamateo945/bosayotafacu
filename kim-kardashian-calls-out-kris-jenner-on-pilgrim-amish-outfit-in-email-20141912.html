Kim Kardashian Calls Out Kris Jenner on Pilgrim, Amish Outfit in Email

Publish date: 2024-07-10

Mom, you’re so embarrassing!

Kim Kardashian dresses to impress, but she also wants her famous family to do the same! Kris Jenner posted an amazing screenshot of an email she received from Kim on Friday, Dec. 19, telling (more like demanding!) her to stop wearing a certain dress style.

“I love you mom but no more pilgrim adams family outfits,” Kardashian, 34, wrote in the message. “You have exhausted this look done. Move on we need chic, tights dresses not this Omish sh–t anymore.”

Jenner, 59, failed to point out her daughter’s misspelling of Amish, but did describe where she wore the supposed drab threads. (Khloe Kardashian’s ex French Montana recently misspelled “congradulations” in a card he sent Kourtney Kardashian and Scott Disick after the birth of their third child.)
