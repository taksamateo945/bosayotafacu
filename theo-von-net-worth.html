Theo Von’s Early Life and Career

Theo Von was born as Theodor Capitani von Kurnatowski on March 19, 1980, in Covington, Louisiana.

Theo Von started his entertainment career at age 19 when he appeared on MTV’s reality show Road Rules: Maximum Velocity Tour in 2000.

Theo Von then pursued stand-up comedy and performed on various shows such as Live at Gotham, The Half Hour, and Kamikaze Comedy.
