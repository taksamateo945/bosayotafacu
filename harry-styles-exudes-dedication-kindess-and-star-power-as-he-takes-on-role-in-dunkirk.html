Harry Styles Exudes Dedication, Kindess, and Star Power as he Takes on Role in Dunkirk

Publish date: 2024-02-07

While Dunkirk isn’t set to be released until July of 2017 and little is known about the cast and the roles they’ll be taking on, there is one thing that everyone anticipating the film has been made very aware of: Harry Styles will be making his film debut in Dunkirk.

Fans of One Direction, upon first hearing the rumors, were immediately floored at the prospect of seeing Harry be part of something so extraordinary.  The Battle of Dunkirk happened in France during World War 2 and a film about a historical moment in time is sure to long be hailed as an important part of the entertainment industry, especially directed by Christoper Nolan.

The film is set to focus on Operation Dynamo, which was the British Military operation that took place between the 27th of May and 4th of June.
