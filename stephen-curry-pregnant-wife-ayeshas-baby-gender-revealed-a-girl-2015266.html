Stephen Curry, Pregnant Wife Ayesha's Baby Gender Revealed: a Girl!

Publish date: 2024-06-20

They’re going to have their hands full! Stephen Curry and his pregnant wife, Ayesha, are adding another little girl to their family. A source exclusively confirms to Us Weekly that the couple are expecting their second daughter together.

The Golden State Warriors player, 27, may have just won the NBA Championship, but all eyes were on his supportive 9-month pregnant wife and daughter Riley this season, who often sat courtside.

Riley, 2, stole the show on several occasions, particularly when she interrupted her famous dad during a press conference, pretended to sneeze in front of reporters, and got shy at the team’s victory parade.
