Kylie Jenner Documents Movie Night With Tyga on Snapchat: Watch

Publish date: 2024-01-30

Kylie Jenner annoys her boyfriend at times. The newly blonde star shared a bunch of Snapchats from her movie night in with boyfriend Tyga on Wednesday, Sept. 2, and proved they’re just a regular couple — well, with a home movie theater in her newly renovated $2.7 million Calabasas, Calif., mansion, that is.

The 18-year-old’s dogs, Norman and Bambi, were also present for their date night. After Tyga raps at and teases one of the dogs, Jenner can be heard saying, “You scared her!” In another video, while comforting her pup, she jokes, “Don’t make me choose, cause I’ll choose her.”

After Tyga returns from another room with Cheez-Its because he couldn’t find popcorn, Jenner whines that she wants to watch Ted 2, but it’s only available for “pre-order.” In the next snap, Jenner starts to annoy her love, who tells her, “You're driving me f—king insane!”

The social media maven, who recently turned 18 last month, documents almost her every move on Snapchat. She and the rapper have been dating since the rapper called it quits with Blac Chyna in August 2014. 

On Thursday, Jenner shared videos from a salon, where she got her newly dyed hair cut and her eyebrows bleached to match.
